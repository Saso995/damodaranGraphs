About Us

My name is Gaetano Barberi, the founder of the website along with Salvatore Bova. I am MSc student in Finance at Bocconi University, I am passionate about value investing and I manage a small fund in my free time. In analysing a company, I like comparing its multiples with the industry averages and I often resort to the data provided by Prof. Aswath Damodaran. Valuator.it has the humble goal to reorganize the data and make them accessible in a more intuitive way. I am responsible for the content of the website. Salvatore is a MSc student in Computer Engineeiring at Politecnico di Milano, he is passionate about cyber security and he's the guy that grants the website runs properly.

About Data

As for now, most of the data displayed in this website has been collected, processed and interpreted by Aswath Damodaran, Professor of Finance at the Stern School of Business at New York University. All the data can be freely accessed on his website. Our aim is to organize those information that come as file excel in their original form, creating interactive representation, improving the accessibility and understandability.

Useful financial information displayed in a very intuitive way using python-based interactive plots and tables. Surf our website and find out about the great varity of information available.

Interactivity

All the plots are interactive. This means that by interacting with the graphs you can decide which piece of information shall be displayed, compare data, take screenshots and more.

Data reliability

All the data are provided by third parties. Our main source is the website of Aswath Damodaran, one of the most known professor in the field of Corporate Finance. Our contribution here consists in reorganizing the data, improving accessibility.
