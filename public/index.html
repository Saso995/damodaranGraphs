About our website

The right place for you!

Whether you are practitioner looking for a specific industry multiple or just a student curious about the profitability of different industries across Europe, this is the right place for you!

What will you find?

Useful financial information displayed in a very intuitive way using python-based interactive plots and tables. Surf our website and find out about the great varity of information available.

Interactivity

All the plots are interactive. This means that by interacting with the graphs you can decide which piece of information shall be displayed, compare data, take screenshots and more.

Data reliability

All the data are provided by third parties. Our main source is the website of Aswath Damodaran, one of the most known professor in the field of Corporate Finance.
